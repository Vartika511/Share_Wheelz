The Car Polling System is a web-based platform designed to address transportation needs by facilitating carpooling among individuals traveling on similar routes. The project aims to reduce traffic congestion, greenhouse gas emissions, and overall environmental impact caused by individual commuting. This full-stack application will enable users to find, connect, and share rides efficiently, promoting sustainable transportation options within the community.

Ride Creation

Registered users can create ride listings, specifying their starting point, destination, time of departure, and the number of available seats in their vehicles.

Ride Search

Users can search for available rides based on preferences, including departure time, pickup/drop-off locations, and ride ratings.

Ride Booking

Interested users can book available rides, and ride owners can review and confirm booking requests.

User Ratings and Reviews

After completing a ride, users can rate and provide feedback on their co-passengers, ensuring a trustworthy community.
